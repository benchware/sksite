Departments and public organizations

Responsibilities, service areas and public information profiles for departments and registered public organizations.

On this page

  • Departments
  • Technology and infrastructure organizations

Departments

Metroville Police Department (MVPD)

Public safety

Responsible for policing, investigation coordination and public safety operations in Metroville.

Metroville Fire Department (MVFD)

Emergency services

Provides fire response, rescue coordination and emergency support services.

Metroville Infrastructure Development (MID)

Infrastructure

A Metroville-government funded department aiming to develop Metroville's infrastructure. MID has made improvements to roads in Metroville as well as pioneering the first metro line for the city. MID is the parent organization of MID Metro as well as PT Telcumsel.

Metroville Education (Education)

Education

Coordinates public education, school administration and education service planning.

Technology and infrastructure organizations

Xenit

Technology and infrastructure corporation

Security, networking and municipal infrastructure technology provider involved in connectivity, surveillance hardware and institutional technology systems.

Areas of activity

  • xOS Urban municipal systems
  • XCS cellular and network equipment
  • Security cameras and tactical recording hardware
  • Xenit Laboratories research and development
  • Xenit Education institutional technology

Breeze Tech

Consumer electronics corporation

Consumer electronics and digital lifestyle company producing devices and home systems used by residents and institutions.

Areas of activity

  • Mobile phones and tablets
  • Displays and home systems
  • Consumer devices connected to municipal networks
  • Retail electronics presence in Metroville

Metroville Infrastructure Development

Government-funded department

Develops public infrastructure for Metroville, including road improvements, metro-line work and public communications projects. Parent organization of MID Metro and PT Telcumsel.

Areas of activity

  • Road improvements
  • MID Metro
  • PT Telcumsel
  • Infrastructure planning and reporting
